### 🌙 The Vision
At **iths.online**, we don't just write code; we craft digital experiences. This is a workspace born out of a passion for the "what if." We focus on creating tools that are as beautiful as they are functional, contributing back to the open-source ecosystem that built the web we love today.

### 🛠️ Our Toolkit
We believe in using the right tool for the job. Our current experiments involve:

| Category | Tech We Love |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Frontend** | `React` `Next.js` `Tailwind CSS` `TypeScript` |
| **Backend** | `Node.js` `Python` `PostgreSQL` `GraphQL` |
| **Infrastructure** | `Docker` `GitHub Actions` `Vercel` `AWS` |
| **Design** | `Figma` `Spline` `Adobe Creative Suite` |

### 🤝 Come Build With Us
We aren't looking for "resources"—we're looking for **humans** with a spark. Whether you're a seasoned developer, a pixel-perfect designer, or a documentation wizard, there's a seat for you at our table.

**How to join the collective:**
1. **Explore:** Look through our [active repositories](https://github.com).
2. **Connect:** Don't just send a resume—tell us a story. What are you building? What makes you excited about code?
3. **Reach Out:** Send a well-designed introduction to **[harinand@iths.online](mailto:harinand@iths.online)**.
